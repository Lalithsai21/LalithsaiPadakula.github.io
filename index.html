ML - Fake News Prediction

In this project, I developed a machine learning model for classifying news articles as real or fake. After dataset exploration, I handled missing values and performed text pre-processing. Using TfidfVectorizer, I converted text into numerical features and chose a logistic regression model for binary classification. The model demonstrated strong generalization with high accuracy scores on both training and testing sets. I showcased its practical application through a predictive system, illustrating its effectiveness in distinguishing between real and fake news.
